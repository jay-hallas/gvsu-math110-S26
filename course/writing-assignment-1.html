    Worksheet Writing Assignment 1
    

    This assignment covers qualitative graphs and visual patterns from Chapter 1.
    🔗
    • For each graph question, explain your reasoning in complete sentences.
      🔗
      🔗
    • For pattern questions, show how you identified the pattern and justify your formula.
      🔗
      🔗
    • Be as detailed as possible in showing your logical reasoning.
      🔗
      🔗
    🔗

    1. Qualitative Graphs: A Pizza’s Journey.

    A frozen pizza is placed into a hot oven to bake. Once it is done, it is left on the counter to cool while the family eats dinner. After dinner, the leftover slices are placed in the refrigerator and left there until the temperature stabilizes. Later that evening, one slice is reheated in the microwave.
    🔗
    On the axes below, sketch a qualitative graph of the pizza’s internal temperature \(T\) over time \(t\text{.}\) Focus on the overall shape and direction of each segment. Draw and label each segment A through E as described in the parts below.
    🔗
    Note: for this assignment, you may use line segments to make your graph.
    🔗

    (a) Segment A: In the oven.

    Draw the segment representing the frozen pizza heating up in the oven. In one sentence, describe what is happening to the temperature.
    🔗
    🔗

    (b) Segment B: Cooling on the counter.

    Draw the segment representing the pizza cooling at room temperature while the family eats. In one sentence, explain why this segment should end at a higher temperature than segment A began.
    🔗
    🔗

    (c) Segment C: Going into the fridge.

    Draw the segment representing the leftover slices cooling down in the refrigerator. In one sentence, explain why this segment should end lower than segment B ended.
    🔗
    🔗

    (d) Segment D: Sitting in the fridge.

    Draw the segment representing the pizza sitting in the refrigerator after it has fully cooled. In one sentence, describe what the graph looks like during this phase and why.
    🔗
    🔗

    (e) Segment E: Reheated in the microwave.

    Draw the segment representing a slice being reheated in the microwave. In one sentence, explain how the speed of reheating compares to the original oven heating in segment A, and how your graph shows that.
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗
